Engage Onboarding

Get started quickly by following the few steps below. Reach support at sales@widgytech.com for further assistance.

1. Add the Widgy script

Add the following script tag to the HTML head on markets where you want to show Widgy Engage.

Click to copy
<script src="https://data.widgytech.com/p/js/widgy.js" type="text/javascript"></script>

Then insert the iframe mount point at the exact location where the experience should appear.

Click to copy
<div id="widgy-iframe"></div>

2. Share your requirements

Once the script is in place, confirm the setup details with the Widgy team so the correct experience can be configured for your brand.

Website domain Please share the domain where the script will be added so we can whitelist it for setup.
Product and layout requirements Please let us know if you have any preferences for product type, placement, sizing, branding, or visual presentation.
Reward trigger Please let us know if you have a preferred reward trigger, such as watching 3 videos or reaching another engagement milestone.
Rules and restrictions Please let us know if there are any rules or restrictions we should take into account, including licensing, compliance, brand safety, or excluded advertisers.

3. Let Widgy configure the experience

After you share the technical placement and commercial requirements, the Widgy team can configure the final setup for your brand on the same platform.

4. Get access and monitor performance

Once live, you will receive access to monitor performance and review the data and revenue.

Need help? Reach out to sales@widgytech.com if you need support during setup.
