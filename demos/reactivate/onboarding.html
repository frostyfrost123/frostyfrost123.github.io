1. Sign the contract

Please sign the Widgy contract first. Once that is completed, you can proceed with the campaign setup steps below.

2. Receive your campaign materials

The Widgy team will provide everything you need to run the campaign, including suggested messaging copy and any graphical assets relevant to the promotion. You can run the campaign across channels such as email, WhatsApp, Telegram, and other relevant channels you control.

Campaign messaging Suggested copy will be shared for you to use across email, WhatsApp, Telegram, and your other campaign channels. You are welcome to adapt the tone to fit your brand voice.
Graphical assets Where applicable, ready-to-use creative assets will be provided alongside the campaign materials.

3. Use the tracking URL

Each campaign comes with a unique URL that must be used for all click-outs. This link ensures the customer interaction is properly tracked and that the correct deal is unlocked for them.

Important Do not modify or substitute this URL. Using a different link will break tracking and prevent the offer from being delivered correctly to the customer.

4. Go live

Once your campaign is in place using the provided materials and tracking URL, the campaign is ready to go. If you want the Widgy team to confirm everything looks correct before launch, please reach out to the Widgy team at onboarding@widgytech.com.

5. Monitor performance

Once live, you will receive login details to the Widgy Dashboard where you can find analytics, monitor performance, and review your data and revenue.

Need help? Reach out to onboarding@widgytech.com if you need support during setup.
