Exchange qualified traffic with brands that can convert it.

Widgy Exchange lets subscription and dating brands unlock value from users who are not ready to pay directly. A customer can get access by subscribing to a partner brand, while your brand also participates as a destination for traffic from the network.

1

Send traffic

Offer Widgy Exchange at moments where users are blocked by payment, access, or subscription friction.

2

Accept traffic

Your brand must also be able to receive qualified users from other participating Exchange partners.

3

Reward access

When the user completes the partner subscription, the original brand grants the promised access period.

What to prepare

Provide the partner brands you want to send traffic to, the access reward you want to offer, and the destination experience where your own brand can accept inbound Exchange traffic.
