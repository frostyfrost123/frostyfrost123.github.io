Engage Onboarding

Get started quickly by following the few steps below. Reach support at onboarding@widgytech.com for further assistance.

1. Sign the contract

Please sign the Widgy contract first. Once that is completed, you can proceed with step 2 below.

2. Add the Widgy script

Add the following script tag to the HTML head on sites where you want to show Widgy Engage.

Click to copy
<script src="https://data.widgytech.com/p/js/widgy.js" type="text/javascript"></script>

Then insert the iframe mount point at the exact location where the experience should appear.

Click to copy
<div id="widgy-iframe"></div>

3. Share your requirements

Once the script is in place, confirm the setup details with the Widgy team by filling in the form below, or reach out to your point of contact to go through the setup together.

4. Let Widgy configure the experience

After you share the technical placement and commercial requirements, the Widgy team can configure the final setup for your brand on the same platform.

5. Acceptance testing -> Live

The Widgy team will reach out when it is time to carry out acceptance testing. If any changes or tweaks are needed, they will be identified and addressed before launch.

6. Get access and monitor performance

Once live, you will receive login details to the Widgy Dashboard where you can find analytics, monitor performance, and review your data and revenue.

Need help? Reach out to onboarding@widgytech.com if you need support during setup.
